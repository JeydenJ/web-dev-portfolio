Introduction

During my current internship at Evaheld, I have been actively involved in developing a unique platform focused on preserving memories and sending personalized messages to loved ones, which are delivered posthumously. This project aims to create a meaningful and secure way for users to leave messages for their family members, ensuring their legacy is preserved. As a part of the development team, my role has been integral in building and maintaining various aspects of the web application.

Role and Contributions

In my role as a web developer at Evaheld, my contributions include:

  • Creating and maintaining webpages using Next.js and React.js, ensuring a seamless and responsive user experience.
  • Fixing bugs and improving the existing codebase to enhance application performance and stability.
  • Constructing templates and components using TypeScript, HTML, and Tailwind CSS to streamline the development process and ensure consistency across the application.
  • Collaborating closely with designers to translate their visual and interaction designs into functional frontend webpages.
  • Implementing SEO best practices to improve the visibility and ranking of the website.
  • Working with Node.js and MongoDB for backend development and data management, ensuring the application is robust and scalable.
  • Using GitHub for version control to manage code changes and collaborate effectively with the team.

Project Overview

The Evaheld project focuses on preserving memories and delivering personalized messages to loved ones after the user has passed away. By leveraging modern web technologies, we aim to create a secure, intuitive, and reliable platform that allows users to leave heartfelt messages for their family members, ensuring their legacy endures.

Target Audience

The target audience for Evaheld includes individuals looking for a meaningful way to preserve their memories and communicate with loved ones beyond their lifetime. Our goal is to provide a thoughtful and user-friendly solution for people who wish to leave a lasting impact.

Results and Impact

Through our dedicated efforts, we have developed a robust platform that effectively meets the needs of our users. The positive feedback from users has highlighted the platform's ease of use and the emotional value it provides. This project has demonstrated the significance of thoughtful design and technical excellence in delivering a product that resonates with users on a personal level.
