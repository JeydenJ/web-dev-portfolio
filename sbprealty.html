Introduction

In my most recent internship, I worked with a team of four designers collaborated to develop a website named www.sbprealty.com.au which focuses on real estate using WordPress and Figma. This project involved creating an engaging and functional website tailored to the needs of real estate professionals. As a web designer, I played a key role in designing the website's layout and ensuring an intuitive user experience.

Role and Contributions

In this project, I utilized my expertise in web design and communication to deliver a comprehensive and user-friendly website. My responsibilities included:

  • Designing wireframes and prototypes in Figma to illustrate the website's structure and visual elements.
  • Creating the website using WordPress, ensuring it was both aesthetically pleasing and functional.
  • Collaborating with three other designers to maintain a consistent design language and adhere to project guidelines.
  • Communicating effectively with various stakeholders, including leasing officers, product managers, and digital marketers, to gather requirements and feedback.
  • Ensuring the website met the specific needs of the real estate industry and the client's objectives.

Project Overview

The Sydney Boutique Property project aimed to provide a comprehensive platform for real estate professionals to showcase properties and engage with potential clients. Leveraging WordPress for its robust content management capabilities and Figma for design precision, we developed a responsive and user-centric website that facilitates seamless property browsing and inquiries.

Target Audience

The target audience for the Sydney Boutique Property includes real estate professionals such as agents and brokers, as well as potential buyers and renters. Our goal was to create a website that effectively showcases properties and provides an excellent user experience for all visitors.

Results and Impact

Through our collaborative efforts, we successfully delivered a fully functional and visually appealing real estate website. Positive feedback from clients emphasized the ease of navigation and the professional look of the site. The project demonstrated the importance of effective communication and teamwork in achieving a polished final product that meets client expectations.
